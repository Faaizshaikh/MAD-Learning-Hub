Mobile App Deployment & Publishing

Learn how to publish your app on Google Play Store and Apple App Store

What is App Deployment?

Understanding the process of releasing your app to users

Definition

App deployment is the process of releasing your mobile application to users through official app stores or direct distribution. It involves preparing the app, meeting platform requirements, and making it available for download.

Pre-Deployment Checklist

  • ‚úì Complete testing and bug fixes
  • ‚úì Update app version number
  • ‚úì Create app icons (all sizes required)
  • ‚úì Prepare app screenshots
  • ‚úì Write app description and keywords
  • ‚úì Set up privacy policy
  • ‚úì Prepare promotional assets
  • ‚úì Review app store guidelines

Distribution Channels

There are multiple ways to distribute your mobile app:

Official App Stores

Publish on Google Play Store (Android) and Apple App Store (iOS) for maximum reach.

Third-party Stores

Amazon Appstore, Samsung Galaxy Store, and other alternative Android stores.

Direct Distribution

Distribute APK directly or through your website (Android only).

Enterprise Distribution

Distribute to employees or organizations through enterprise programs.

Publishing on Apple App Store

Steps to publish your iOS app

Step-by-Step Process

üí≥

Step 1: Apple Developer Account

Enroll in Apple Developer Program ($99/year). You'll need an Apple ID, legal name, and payment method.

üì±

Step 2: Create App ID

Create a unique App ID in Apple Developer account that matches your app's bundle identifier.

üîê

Step 3: Generate Certificates

Create code signing certificates and provisioning profiles needed to sign your app for release.

üì¶

Step 4: Build Archive

Build an Archive from Xcode using your release configuration and signing certificates.

üìù

Step 5: Create App Record

Go to App Store Connect and create a new app with your App ID, name, default language, and bundle ID.

üñºÔ∏è

Step 6: Add App Screenshots

Add screenshots for iPhone and iPad (minimum 2, maximum 5 per screen size) and upload a promotional image.

üìù

Step 7: Fill Store Information

Add app description, keywords, support URL, privacy policy, and other required information.

‚≠ê

Step 8: Set Ratings & Pricing

Select your app's content rating category, set pricing for different countries, and configure in-app purchases if needed.

üì§

Step 9: Upload Build

Use Xcode or Transporter to upload your Archive. TestFlight allows beta testing before App Store release.

‚öñÔ∏è

Step 10: Review & Submit

Review all information and submit for review. Apple reviews apps within 24-48 hours but may take longer.

Important Requirements

Minimum iOS Version: iOS 11.0 or higher (varies)
App Icon: 1024x1024 px required
Privacy Policy: Required if app collects user data
Reviewer Guidelines: Strictly reviewed for compliance
Testing: TestFlight available for internal and external testing
Review Time: Usually 24-48 hours, can take longer
