## ğŸ’¡ Key Concepts Summary

### 3 Types of Apps
- **Native:** Platform-specific, best performance
- **Hybrid:** One code, multiple platforms
- **Web:** Browser-based, no installation

### 2 Major Platforms
- **Android:** 70% market share, multiple devices
- **iOS:** 27% market share, premium devices

### Development Lifecycle
1. Idea & Planning
2. Design & Prototyping
3. Development
4. Testing & QA
5. Deployment
6. Maintenance & Updates

### Popular Tools
- **Android Studio:** Android native development
- **Flutter:** Cross-platform, fast development
- **React Native:** JavaScript-based, huge community
- **Firebase:** Backend services and analytics
